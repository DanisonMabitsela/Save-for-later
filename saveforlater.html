Article

Pancakes are a tasty breakfast food that can be customized with various flavors and toppings. To make pancakes, mix basic ingredients like flour, sugar, eggs, milk, and butter, and cook on a non-stick pan until golden brown. Serve with butter, syrup, or other toppings of your choice. Pancakes are a simple and delicious way to start your day.

pan cake image

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Additional butter or cooking spray for cooking

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and whisk until just combined. Do not overmix, a few lumps are okay.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Once hot, add a small amount of butter or cooking spray.
  5. Use a 1/4 cup measure to pour batter onto the pan. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake and the edges start to look set, about 2-3 minutes. Flip the pancake and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes on the other side.
  6. Repeat with remaining batter, adding additional butter or cooking spray to the pan as needed.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.
